SWEET TARTS CAFE

Come on in and enjoy a freshly brewed cup of coffee. We have a wide variety of different hot, cold, and seasonal drinks to choose from! We recommend also trying out one of our manyfreshly baked pastries, delivered from nearby local bakeries.



Sweet Tarts Cafe has been a local cafe that has been around since the 1980's. With it's newly modern and re-designed interior, it is a great place to grab a quick bite for on-the-go or hanging out with your friends for an outdoor brunch during the sunny days! Come on in to Sweet Tarts Cafe and help us make your day a bit brighter!
